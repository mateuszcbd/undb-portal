SOI Mission

To provide a global platform to build partnerships and enhance capacity to achieve the Aichi Biodiversity Targets related to marine and coastal biodiversity in a holistic manner by:

  • Achieving a balance between sustainable use and conservation and the promotion of flexible and diverse approaches;
  • Identifying best practices, facilitating information sharing, and learning from experiences;
  • Creating partnerships that can provide for targeted capacity building, training, technical assistance and learning exchange;
  • Providing for two-way communication among policymakers, scientific communities and local stakeholders;
  • Monitoring progress towards the Aichi Biodiversity Targets on marine and coastal biodiversity (particularly Targets 6, 10 and 11);
  • Facilitating the provision of guidance and guidelines that will help their achievement; and
  • Improving the scientific basis for implementation.

SOI Global Partnership Meeting and High-Level Meeting at CBD COP 12

The Sustainable Ocean Initiative will be holding two important meetings on the margins of the 12th meeting of the Conference of the Parties to the CBD (COP 12). The SOI Global Partnership Meeting will be held in Seoul, Republic of Korea, 3-4 October 2014. The SOI High-Level Meeting will be held in Pyeongchang, Republic of Korea on 16 October 2014.
