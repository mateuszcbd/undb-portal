SOI Website Launched

The Sustainable Ocean Initiative has launched this website as a means to improve information-sharing and awareness regarding the activities of SOI and its many partners, and to facilitate connectivity and coordination among SOI partners.

SOI Regional Capacity-building Workshop for South America convened in Lima, Peru

The CBD Secretariat, in collaboration with the Ministry of Environment of Peru, the Permanent Commission for the South Pacific (CPPS) and various other partners, organized a regional capacity building workshop for South America in Lima, Peru from 23 to 27 February 2015.

 

SOI Action Plan 2015-2020 launched at High-Level at CBD COP 12

The SOI High-Level Meeting, held on the margins of the 12th meeting of the Conference of the Parties to the CBD (COP 12) in October 2014 in Pyeongchang, Republic of Korea, welcomed a new action plan for the Sustainable Ocean Initiative. This SOI Action Plan 2015-2020 was developed during the SOI Global Partnership Meeting, held in Seoul from 3 to 4 October 2014 and lays out an ambitious set of capacity building activities in the coming years.
