Welcome

Living in Harmony with Nature - the United Nations Decade on Biodiversity contributes to the implementation of the Strategic Plan for Biodiversity 2011-2020.

"The Sustainable Ocean Initiative provides a key opportunity to address capacity gaps by building partnerships that can provide for targeted capacity building, training, technical assistance and learning exchange. I encourage you all to share the vision and mission of the Substainable Ocean Initiative and contribute to a substantial future for our oceans for generations to come."
Braulio Ferriera de Souza Dias, Executive Secretary, Convention on Biological Diversity
"[SOI] has helped us build capacity as well as bring in a collaborative approach, regionally and globally. Support such as this and our own programmes gives us real hope that we will be able to meet most of the Aichi Biodiversity Targets by 2020."
Barbara Thompson, Deputy Minister of Environmental Affairs, South Africa
"The task is enormous, as are the challenges. They are as big as the ocean is deep. We must rise to those challenges, and SOI is part of our common response."
Xavier Sticker, Ambassador for Environment, France
"We want to reaffirm our commitment to the ecosystem approach in the management of our biological diversity and the maintenance of ecosystem services, trusting that through cooperation initiatives, like SOI and other partners, [we will be able to] to achieve it."
Marco Tax, Executive Sub-Secretary, National Council of Protected Areas, Guatemala
