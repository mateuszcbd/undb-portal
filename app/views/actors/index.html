Actors of the United Nations Decade on Biodiversity

Safeguarding biodiversity calls for the active involvement of all citizens, businesses, governments and organizations. Collectively, we need to build a vast network to communicate the importance of biodiversity with the rest of world. Very substantial changes from business-as-usual trends are needed in order to address the challenges we face: to slow and stop the loss of biodiversity.

Partners to the United Nations Decade on Biodiversity are making a long-term commitment to promote biodiversity in all their activities and communications. They are committing to take action for positive change.

We are looking to partner with those who work at the international, national or local levels, who do work related to safeguarding biodiversity, and would like to publicize their information products and activities through this website.

There are different categories of partnerships:
